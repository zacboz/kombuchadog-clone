OUR KOMBUCHA

Kombucha is thought to have originated two thousand years ago. When properly brewed, kombucha is a tasty, low sugar, naturally carbonated, fermented beverage that contains a potent assortment of antioxidants and probiotics.

AUTHENTIC

Authentic Kombucha is 21+! The traditional recipe for kombucha calls for fermenting one gallon of tea with one cup of sugar. If fermented properly and for long enough, the resulting beverage will contain about 6 grams of sugar per 8 oz of tea (not sweet), and contain about 1.4% ABV. This is considered an alcoholic beverage as defined by US law.

UNDILUTED

In order to make kombucha below the legal limit of 0.5% ABV, we would have to dilute ours by 60-70%. When you drink Kombucha Dog, you can be sure that you are getting 100% of the anti-oxidants and probiotics that you deserve.

RAW AND ORGANIC

The ingredients used in our kombucha are hand selected. We only use organic tea, raw turbinado sugar, and cold pressed juices. What you get is a gluten-free, vegan, raw, never pasteurized, BPA-free, non- GMO, organic experience.
